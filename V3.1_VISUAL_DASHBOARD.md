## AI Prompt Changes

### New Output Format

```markdown
## DASHBOARD METRICS
- Total Fallacies: 3
- Objectivity: 7
- Legitimacy: Medium

## FALLACY INSTANCES

**Fallacy:** Cherry Picking
**Confidence:** 85%
**Location:** "Crime up 15% under current administration"
**Why Misleading:** Selects specific time period ignoring longer trends
**Biases Exploited:** Confirmation bias, anchoring bias, in-group bias
**Better Version:** "Crime rates fluctuate year-to-year; 5-year trend shows..."
---

**Fallacy:** False Causality  
**Confidence:** 90%
**Location:** "After new policy, unemployment dropped"
**Why Misleading:** Ignores economic recovery already in progress
**Biases Exploited:** Illusory correlation, narrative fallacy
**Better Version:** "Unemployment dropped during this period, influenced by..."
---

## CRITICAL THINKING

1. What other factors might explain these trends?
2. Are comparison time periods fair and representative?
3. What sources would provide contrasting perspectives?

## FACT-CHECK

- Bureau of Labor Statistics for employment data
- Census Bureau for demographic information
- FactCheck.org for political claim verification
```

### Key Changes
1. **Separate instances**: Each fallacy occurrence gets its own entry
2. **Concise**: One sentence explanations
3. **Structured**: Easy to parse with regex
4. **Separator**: `---` between instances
